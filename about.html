TOP SCORE SPORTY LEAGUE HISTORY

The Sports Science Company was incorporated with a Vision to apply Science to the domain of Sports which helps early stage emerging athletes’ and build tomorrows sports legacies. In-house engineers and scientist are involved in designing and integrating software into various sports to help coaches, athletes, and map player’s performance and allow them to perform better. The Company is the brain child of Mr. Syed Zakir, an Electronics Engineer & the Managing Director of the company with a vast experience of 25+ years in the domain of Applied Science in Leisure and Sports.
